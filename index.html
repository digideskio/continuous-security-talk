Speed matters

Traditional ops where deployments take entire weeks
aren't acceptable anymore.

To compete, startups need fast release cycles.


15min from patch to prod is the new standard!

Doing things fast means taking risks

Your typical cross-team interaction goes like this:

  • Product: "feat. X should have gone live A WEEK AGO!"
  • Security: "it's insecure, we need time to review and test."

Security
must be part of the Product

  • Be a member of the DevOps team
  • Understand the roadmap
  • Share the successes
  • Share the failures
  • Write code that makes things better

DevOps: Dev+Ops

At a human level, it's about easier communication between devs and ops.
